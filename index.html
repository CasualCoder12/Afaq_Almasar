Company Profile

A Professional Trading Enterprise in the Heart of the Kingdom

Afaq Almasar Alhadithah Company LLC — آفاق المسار الحديثة — is a Saudi Arabian limited liability company incorporated and operating in Madinah Munawara. The company was established in 2026 to participate actively in the Kingdom's growing commercial landscape through general trading and a broad range of procurement and supply services.

The company name, translating to "Horizons of the Modern Path," reflects our forward-looking approach to business: embracing modern commercial practices, ethical standards, and professional service delivery to build a sustainable trading enterprise that contributes to Saudi Arabia's economic growth.

As a one-person company (Establishment with LLC structure), Afaq Almasar Alhadithah operates with focus and agility, allowing for swift decision-making, personalized service, and a close working relationship with each client and supplier. Our lean structure is backed by broad commercial knowledge and a commitment to operational excellence.

We serve a diverse clientele including businesses seeking wholesale suppliers, companies requiring procurement support, distributors looking for product sourcing, and partners interested in collaborative trading arrangements within and beyond Saudi Arabia.
